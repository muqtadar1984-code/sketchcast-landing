About

The company behind SketchCast

SketchCast is built by Aethel Twin Sdn. Bhd., the Malaysian AI company incorporated in 2026 and awarded Malaysia Digital (MD) status by MDEC in May 2026.

SketchCast turns a school's own textbooks into complete teaching kits — narrated lesson videos, worksheets, exams and lesson plans — and wraps them in the operating layer a school actually runs on: timetabling, absences, analytics, a study coach for students and a parent portal.

Why we built it

Why we built SketchCast

Every AI teaching tool generates from prompts or from its own content library. Teachers don't teach from either — they teach from the book the school adopted. SketchCast starts there: index the school's own textbook, and produce lessons sized to a real class period, in the sequence the curriculum already follows.

“For me the test was simple: if it doesn't begin with the book a teacher already trusts, it isn't really helping — so that's where we made SketchCast begin.”

— Muqtadar Quraishi, Founder & CEO
